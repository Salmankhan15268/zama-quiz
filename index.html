Zama Questions & Answers

Q1. What is Zama?

A1: Zama is an open-source cryptography company building products based on Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) for blockchains and AI.

Q2. What is Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)?

A2: FHE allows computations on encrypted data without decrypting it, ensuring full privacy.

Q3. What tools does Zama provide?

A3: TFHE-rs, Concrete, and Concrete ML.

Q4. What can developers build with Zama?

A4: Confidential smart contracts, privacy-preserving dApps, and encrypted AI models.

Q5. How can someone get started with Zama?

A5: Follow Quick Start guides, install libraries, and use Solidity & Relayer SDK examples.
